## Summary

Made the Act 2 span-annotation scene interactive (the Act 2 equivalent of Act 1's M0-T08). A new
`Act2EntityExtractionInteractionController` owns one `EntityExtractionSession` plus the selected chip
and a chipKey→type map; the presenter wires word chips and palette items to it and renders
selected/tagged visuals. Clicking a chip selects it; clicking an entity type tags the selected chip
(adds an `EntitySpan`); clicking a tagged chip untags it (removes the span). The Validate button
remains a disabled placeholder. All add/remove route through `EntityExtractionSession`; the Act 2 pure
logic is unchanged.
